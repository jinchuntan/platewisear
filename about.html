PlateWise AR

See how small food choices connect to SDG 12.

Purpose

A short WebAR experience for awareness and reflection on food waste — not real food recognition or behaviour tracking.

SDG 12

Responsible Consumption & Production. Target 12.3 aims to halve per-person food waste at retail and consumer levels by 2030.

What it shows

AR visualization
A 3D plate, food, and action visuals overlaid on the Hiro marker, with spatial text and an SDG 12 label.
Interaction
Browse facts and choose what to do with leftovers — Throw, Save, Share, or Compost — and see the result.
Educational content
Food-waste statistics from official sources, plus a quick quiz and a personal pledge.
How it works
  • Scan the printed Hiro marker.
  • Read a fact, then choose an action.
  • See AR feedback on your choice.
  • Finish with a quick check and a pledge.

Limitations
  • Marker-based AR, not real food recognition.
  • No AI features in this version — planned as future work.
  • Statistics are simplified for learning.
  • Camera needs HTTPS or localhost.
