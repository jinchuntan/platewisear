PlateWise AR

Food-waste images become AR learning exhibits.

Purpose

Scan a curated food-waste image and a short AR exhibit explains what it represents, why it matters for SDG 12, and what you can do. Inspired by food-waste museum concepts that turn discarded food into moments of reflection.

SDG 12

Responsible Consumption & Production. Target 12.3 aims to halve per-person food waste at retail and consumer levels by 2030.

What it shows

AR visualization
A floating exhibit card anchored to the scanned image — title, SDG 12 tag, a statistic, and a recommended-action badge, with a highlight frame.
Interaction
A contextual bottom sheet appears on detection. Choose Throw, Save, Share, or Compost, and tap “Ask more” for a short explanation.
Educational content
Food-waste statistics from official sources, target-specific guidance, plus a quick quiz and a personal pledge.
How it works
  • Scan a curated food-waste image.
  • An AR exhibit appears over it.
  • Choose an action and read the guidance.
  • Finish with a quick check and a pledge.

Limitations
  • Recognises curated image targets only — not arbitrary food photos.
  • No AI yet — image analysis is planned future work.
  • Does not confirm food safety.
  • No exact weight or carbon estimates.
  • Camera needs HTTPS or localhost.

A future version may use AI to analyse your own food-waste photos. For now, PlateWise uses curated image targets included in the app.
