Troubleshooting

  • Make sure the page is opened over HTTPS or on localhost.
  • Check that the browser has camera permission for this site (tap the address-bar icon).
  • Close other apps or tabs that may be using the camera.
  • Use Chrome or Firefox on Android for best results; some iOS browsers limit camera access.
  • Reload the page and allow the camera when prompted.
