How to Use the Marker

  1. Print clearly in black and white on plain white paper.
  2. Do not crop the black border — AR.js needs the full black frame to detect it.
  3. Keep the marker flat and well-lit — avoid bends, glare, and deep shadows.
  4. Scan from around 20–40 cm away with the camera roughly square to the marker.
  5. Open the AR Experience on your phone and point the camera at the marker.

No printer? Display this page on a laptop or tablet screen and scan it with your phone (lower the screen brightness if there is glare).

Tips for Best Results

  • Print at a reasonable size — at least 8 cm × 8 cm (the print layout uses ~10 cm).
  • Ensure even lighting — avoid glare and deep shadows on the marker.
  • Hold your phone about 20–40 cm from the marker.
  • Keep the marker flat and do not bend, fold, or crop it.
