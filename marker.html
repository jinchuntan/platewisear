SDG 12 — Responsible Consumption & Production

This is the standard Hiro marker pattern used by AR.js.
Keep the marker flat, well-lit, and avoid covering the black border.

Tips for Best Results

  • Print on white paper at a reasonable size (at least 8 cm × 8 cm).
  • Ensure even lighting — avoid glare and deep shadows on the marker.
  • Hold your phone about 20–40 cm from the marker.
  • Keep the marker flat and do not bend or fold it.

Alternative: Use This Image on Another Screen

If you cannot print, display this page on a laptop or tablet screen and scan it with your phone camera. Reduce screen brightness glare if needed.
