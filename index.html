🍽️ PlateWise AR

A Mobile WebAR Application for Food Waste Awareness under SDG 12

PlateWise AR is an educational prototype that uses Augmented Reality to help you explore how everyday food-waste decisions connect to environmental impact, household responsibility, and sustainable behaviour.

Point your phone camera at a printed marker to see an interactive AR scene — then choose what to do with leftover food and learn how your choices relate to SDG 12: Responsible Consumption and Production.

📷 Start AR Experience 🖨️ Print / View Marker 🖥️ Try Non-AR Demo Mode ℹ️ About the Project

How It Works

  1. Print or display the PlateWise marker.
  2. Tap Start AR Experience and allow camera access.
  3. Point your camera at the marker — an AR scene will appear.
  4. Navigate food-waste facts and choose an action.
  5. Complete the quiz & reflection.

SDG 12 — Responsible Consumption and Production

This prototype is aligned with United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 12 and its target 12.3, which aims to halve per-capita global food waste at retail and consumer levels by 2030.
